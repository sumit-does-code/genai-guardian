Why Ethics in AI Matters

As AI becomes part of everyday life, the rules we build into it today shape tomorrow's world. GenAI Guardian shows that safety and helpfulness can go hand in hand.

01
Transparency First

Users should always know why a request was blocked and what the system is doing.

02
Harm Prevention

Stop harmful content before it reaches processing — don't just react after the damage is done.

03
Helpful by Design

Ethical limits should make AI more trustworthy and useful, not less helpful.
